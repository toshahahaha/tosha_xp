About Me

Hi, I'm Tosha! I work in technical sales for IBM, specializing in AI portfolio, business automation, and business analytics. I'm passionate about connecting cutting-edge technology with real business value, and I love building things that blend creativity with function.

With a background spanning design and technology, I've developed a deep appreciation for interfaces and experiences that are both beautiful and purposeful. Whether I'm demonstrating IBM's latest AI capabilities or crafting a compelling visual story for a client, I bring the same attention to detail and creativity to everything I do.

When I'm not exploring the latest in enterprise AI, you might find me experimenting with creative projects, staying on top of trends in UX/UI design, or finding new ways to make complex technology feel approachable and human.
