🎵 Spotify → Tosha XP

One-time setup: connect your Spotify account to get a refresh token for the WMP playlist sync.

Step 1: Click the button below to log in with Spotify
Step 2: Copy the refresh token that appears
Step 3: Paste it into your Netlify environment variables as SPOTIFY_REFRESH_TOKEN
Done! You can close this page. The token never expires unless you revoke access.
